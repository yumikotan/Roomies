      Compatibility & Ideas

      This tab can hold your compatibility test results and tips for better roommate living.

      Tips

      Conflict Resolution

      Use "I" statements:

      Express your feelings from your own perspective without placing blame. For example, say "I feel frustrated because..." instead of "You always...".

      Focus on the issue:

      Don't bring up past mistakes. Stay focused on the current conflict and the behavior, not the person.

      Shared Space Etiquette

      Respect boundaries:

      Don't invade other people's physical or personal space. Pay attention to social cues and avoid interrupting others who are focused on work.

      Respect privacy:

      Be mindful of others' conversations and personal business. Avoid loud, private conversations in public spaces.

      Clean up after yourself:

      Keep shared spaces tidy by cleaning up immediately after using them. Don't leave dishes in the sink or personal items in common areas.

      Communication Tips

      Set expectations early:

      Discuss household rules, chores, and boundaries when you first move in together to avoid misunderstandings later.

      Have regular check-ins:

      Schedule monthly household meetings to discuss any concerns, share updates, and address issues before they become big problems.

      Be direct but kind:

      Don't let small annoyances build up. Address concerns promptly with respect and kindness rather than passive-aggressive behavior.

      Financial Management

      Track shared expenses:

      Use the Expenses tab to log all shared costs like groceries, utilities, and household supplies to ensure fair splitting.

      Pay bills on time:

      Set reminders for your share of rent and utilities. Late payments can strain relationships and affect everyone's credit.

      Discuss big purchases:

      Before buying shared items like furniture or appliances, get everyone's input and agreement on the expense.

      Daily Living

      Be mindful of noise:

      Keep noise levels reasonable, especially during early mornings and late nights. Use headphones when others are sleeping or studying.

      Share responsibilities:

      Divide household chores fairly using the Chores tab. Rotate unpleasant tasks so no one feels burdened.

      Respect guests policy:

      Always give roommates notice before having guests over. Agree on quiet hours and overnight guest limits in advance.

      Label your food:

      Mark your personal groceries clearly and respect others' labeled items. Discuss which items are shared and which are personal.
